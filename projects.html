Highlight Video Generator

Generates videos from daily or weekly highligts for video games such as Overwatch and Counter Strike for people to watch. Highlights from Reddit and Twitch are filtered for their quality and rendered it to a video with transitions. Optionally the videos can also be uploaded to a YouTube channel for people to enjoy.

Simple YouTube API

Simple YouTube API is a YouTube API wrapper that supports uploading, fetching and updating videos. Other features are also supported such as channels and comments with more on the way.
